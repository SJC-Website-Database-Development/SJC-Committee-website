Catholic Women's League

For God and Canada — St. Joseph's, Port Hawkesbury

About Our CWL

St. Joseph C.W.L. received their charter from the National Office of the C.W.L. in June, 2001. Next year we will be celebrating our 25th Anniversary. At the present time we have 28 members. We always extend an open invitation to all women of the parish to join us.

With the exception of June, July and August, we meet on the first Sunday of every month after the 10:00 am mass.

The mission statement of the C.W.L. is, "to call its members to grow in faith and to witness the love of God through ministry and service." As members of St. Joseph C.W.L., our goal is to follow this mission statement by serving our parishioners and leading them in faith whenever we are called upon to do so.

What We Do

Funeral Receptions

One of our most important services is hosting funeral receptions whenever families make this request. We feel this gives family members an opportunity to gather and receive support, which is an important part of their healing process after the death of a loved one.

Catering & Community

We cater to priests and deacons of the diocese by providing meals for their meetings and general assemblies. When asked, we also cater to other community organizations.

Student Bursaries

Each year we donate bursaries to two graduating students at SAERC. These students are selected by the school administration.

St. Joseph's Closet

We purchase sneakers for St. Joseph's Closet, which are distributed each year in August before the new school year begins.

Christmas Giving

Instead of exchanging gifts at Christmas time, we choose a service organization in the community, and our members bring in items that this organization can distribute to individuals in need.

50/50 Monthly Draw

In June of 2022, we started a 50/50 monthly ticket draw. From the revenue received, we have pledged $100,000.00 to the St. Joseph Vision 2020 project and contributed to the upkeep of the kitchen in the parish hall.
